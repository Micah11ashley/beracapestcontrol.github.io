About Beraca Pest Control

Our story began on 2020 along the peaceful street of Sto. Niño, Marikina City, when Noel A. Merabona created a pest control service in the midst of Pandemic. Inspired by the biblical place called “Berakah” that means “Valley of Blessings”, the Beraca Pest Control Company is born with the mission to provide a pest-free community that will start in everyone’s individual homes.

Our Mission

Our ultimate objective is to be known as the best "Pest Control" service company in the industry, with the mission of protecting you, your family, and your important possessions with highly trained pest management professionals. We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by cultivating long-term partnerships based on high-quality service and customer care.

Our Vision

We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by cultivating long-term partnerships based on high-quality service and customer care. By using an Integrated Pest Management strategy to pest control, we aim to be the most dependable and trustworthy pest control services company in the industry.
