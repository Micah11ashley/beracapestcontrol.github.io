Interesting Facts About Roaches

We all know for sure that Cockroaches are disgusting. And obviously, nobody wants to see them anywhere. Although roaches aren't everyone's favorite bug, they sure do have some interesting characteristics that people are unaware of, and these characteristics are what helps them survive.

And here are 5 facts about them that you may want to check:

1. THEY ARE AN INVASIVE SPECIES
- The most common roaches that we are seeing everywhere are called "Periplaneta americana" or usually known as American Cockroach, but they didn't actually came from America, instead they came from Africa,they infested the boats towards America in 1600 and it's been spreading to almost every corner in the world since then. Branding them the most successful invasive species on Earth. This fact is not to boost them, but instead we should know that we really need to get rid of these insects.

2. YOU REALLY CAN'T SEE MOST OF THE COCKROACH THAT IS IN YOUR HOME
- You might've seen some numbers of roaches crawling around your home, but that is just a few numbers of them! Cockroaches are nocturnal, hiding in cracks and holes all through the day. If you see one or two cockroaches, there's a good probability you have a secret nest with hundreds of them. If cockroaches are discovered, you must act fast to prevent them from reproducing. Contact us now!

3. ROACHES CAN EAT ANYTHING AND THEY WILL DO SO
- Cockroaches devour organic waste and operate as garbage disposals in nature. Their genetic constitution, combined with the bacteria in their bodies, allows them to eat almost anything and go for lengthy periods of time without eating — some for as long as 2-3 months. This is one of the more vexing facts, because it is one of the characteristics that makes them such a nuisance to homeowners since they can eat anything from your food to your papers! Yikes! You might want to seal your paperworks now.

4. ROACHES ARE PROFESSIONAL SWIMMERS
- You might think that drowning them in water or flushing them down the drainage will kill them, but no! Cockroaches can hold its breath under water for 40 minutes! These bugs can even withstand a half-hour submersion in water. They frequently hold their breath to assist regulate their water loss. But those pests that you flushed down might've created a nest under your sink or toilet!

5. THEY ARE NATURAL SURVIVORS!
- We are not trying to praise them, but honestly these roaches are really hard to kill! They can actually survive anything even a radioactive environment! Have we mentioned that they can live for a week without it's head? That's true! Due to their circulatory system, they don't really bleed out from the injuries that they are getting, infact they will seal their wounds instantly, same goes with their neck and other body parts, but since they can't regenerate their heads, they will die due to starvation. But that is just one thing! Do you know that roaches can also live without food for one month?
These facts prove that cockroaches are some of the most adaptable creatures on earth, which makes controlling and eliminating a cockroach infestation all the more difficult. To get rid of the infestation of cockroaches in your home, contact us!
